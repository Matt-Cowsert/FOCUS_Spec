# FOCUS Outputs

The FOCUS Working Group maintains and releases the following outputs:

1. **FOCUS Specification**: an English language representation of normative requirements, output in HTML and PDF.
2. **FOCUS Requirements Model**: a machine-readable representation of normative requirements from the FOCUS Specification, output in JSON.

These outputs are governed by the FOCUS working group operating procedures, which are documented [here](https://github.com/FinOps-Open-Cost-and-Usage-Spec/foundation/blob/main/operating_procedures.md).
